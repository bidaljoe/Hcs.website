About HCAS Limited

HICOMFORT AND AIR SOLUTIONS (HCAS) LIMITED is a leading HVAC company based in Kampala, Uganda, specializing in air conditioning, ventilation, and refrigeration solutions.

With over 15 years of experience in the industry, we've built a reputation for excellence, reliability, and innovative climate control solutions.

Our team of certified technicians is committed to delivering top-notch service that meets international standards while understanding local needs.
